    How long does a Gel Manicure last?

    Gel manicures typically last between 2 to 3 weeks. The exact duration can vary depending on factors like nail growth, daily activities, and how well the manicure is cared for. Factors like exposure to water, harsh chemicals, and physical activities can shorten the lifespan of a gel manicure.

  • ◆

    What are Dip Powder Nails?

    Dip powder nails, also known as SNS nails, are a type of manicure where a pigmented powder is used to color the nails instead of traditional nail polish or gel. The process involves applying a base coat, dipping the nail into a colored powder, and then sealing it with a topcoat. This technique is known for its durability and long-lasting finish, often lasting up to four weeks.
